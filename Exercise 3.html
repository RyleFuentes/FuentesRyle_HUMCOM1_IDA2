HOW TO ASSEMBLE A PC

STEPS:

Things to keep in mind:
  • Know the purpose of your build
  • Know your budget limit
  • Know the time period that you'll be needing it
Step by step procedure:
  1. Unbox all the parts that you'll need
  2. Start with the motherboard, scan through the build of the motherboard
  3. Insert the processor of your choice into the CPU slot
  4. After inserting properly, connect the fan cooler
  5. Make sure to check if there's a pre-applied thermal paste in the cooler. If there's none apply some directly to the top of the processor
  6. After putting the fan in place and screwing it in, plug its connector to the motherboard.
  7. Next step is to put the RAM into the slots
    8. *check the manual first usually you need to put it in a specific way or else it won't work*
  8. Set up the chassis that you'll be using
  9. Make sure that the motherboard is placed properly then screw it in
  10. Place the power supply(at the back usually of the chassis)then select the wires that you'll need.
    11. *Normally you'll just need one connector to connect to either HDD or SSD, and one connector to the motherboard*
  11. Place the physical storage that you'll be using(e.g...M.2, SSD, HDD)
  12. Connect it to the power supply then use a SATA cable to connect it to the motherboard(Not the case for M.2).
  13. After all that place your GPU of choice into the motherboard
    14. *This is optional because there are CPU's that comes with integrated GPU's*
  14. After all of that is done you are almost done
  15. The last part is compose of software related steps the first one is to get a bootable drive and use it to install an OS to your PC
  16. Lastly check the drivers
    17. *You may be required to install your drivers and chipsets manually depending on what CPU you're using*



That's the end of the procedure, if you're still not comfortable building one for yourself then just go to some lock computer shops in your area
