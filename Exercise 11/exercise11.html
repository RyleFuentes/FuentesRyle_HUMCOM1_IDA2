My Favorite Things

Anime

Watching anime has been one of my hobbies as I grew up. I was able to learn lots of lessons from it and it's been one of my coping mechanisms. One of my favorites are One piece, Hunter x Hunter, Reborn, etc., Anime is not just as simple as fiction for me, they shaped me to become who I am today.

Music

Music connects a lot of people all over the world. Nowadays we use music as a medium for our various emotions. Same as through with me, music has helped me release a lot of my emotions most of them are negative. Music is also my lifeline at school, with the help of it I was able to get a scholarship at university.

Games

Online gaming is a big trend right now, may it be for pc, smartphone, or other platforms. Games enables its players to imagine that they are living in an alternate world, a world of fantasy. The reason why games is part of this list is quite simple, I'm one of those people, games makes me feel more alive, and it helps me release lots of stress just by playing for a couple of hours.
