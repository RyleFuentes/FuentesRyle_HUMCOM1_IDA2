ONE PIECE

Written by: Eiichiro Oda

"Fame, knowledge, strength..." These are some of the most iconic lines and words that you'll see if you've read One Piece before. One Piece is a story of a young pirate who wants to become the King of Pirates someday, however, achieving this dream is far more difficult than he thought it would be. One Piece is a lovely story of this young pirate's journey and the friends that he meets along the way.
